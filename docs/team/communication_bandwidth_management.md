# Communication Bandwidth Management

**Last Updated:** 2026-04-18

> "Assume good intent. Manage bandwidth as a team."

## Our Compact
- **Default to asynchronous** (GitLab comments, Notion).
- **Only schedule a meeting if:**
  1. There is a decision to be made, *and*
  2. Async discussion has stalled.

## Slack Etiquette
- Use threads. Always.
- `@channel` is for SEV1 incidents only.
- Statuses are respected – if someone is in "Focus Mode", do not disturb.

## Reducing Noise
- Weekly "Noise Audit" – identify top 3 interrupt sources and fix one.
- Unsubscribe from noisy GitHub repos and Jira projects you don't actively work on.
