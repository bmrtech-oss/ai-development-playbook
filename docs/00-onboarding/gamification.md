# Gamification System

**Last Updated:** 2026-04-18  
**Owner:** Community Team

## Overview

The Playbook Mastery system gamifies contributions to encourage engagement and learning. Earn badges for different types of contributions and track your progress on the leaderboard.

## How It Works

- **Automatic Tracking:** GitHub Actions monitor PRs, issues, and stars.
- **Daily Updates:** Leaderboard refreshes every 24 hours.
- **Badge System:** See `BADGES.md` for available badges and criteria.

## Getting Started

1. Make your first contribution (open an issue or PR).
2. Check `LEADERBOARD.md` to see your progress.
3. Aim for higher badges by contributing to core areas like ADRs or runbooks.

## Benefits

- **Recognition:** Public acknowledgment of your expertise.
- **Learning:** Encourages exploration of different playbook areas.
- **Community:** Builds a network of playbook practitioners.

## Tips

- Start with small contributions to earn your first badge.
- Focus on areas that interest you most.
- Share your achievements in discussions!
