## Overview

Stay updated with the AI Development Playbook through our weekly podcast. Each episode covers recent changes, new practices, and community highlights.

## How to Subscribe

### Apple Podcasts
1. Open Apple Podcasts app
2. Search for "AI Development Playbook"
3. Subscribe and download episodes

### Spotify
1. Open Spotify app
2. Search for "AI Development Playbook Podcast"
3. Follow the show

### RSS Feed
Subscribe directly to our RSS feed: `https://github.com/bmrtech-oss/ai-development-playbook/raw/main/podcast.xml`

## Episode Schedule

- **Frequency:** Weekly (Mondays)
- **Length:** 10-15 minutes
- **Topics:** Playbook updates, AI engineering tips, community stories
