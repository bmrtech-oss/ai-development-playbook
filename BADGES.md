# Playbook Badges

**Last Updated:** 2026-04-18  
**Owner:** Community Team

## Badge Guide

Earn badges by contributing to the AI Development Playbook. Badges are automatically assigned based on your activity.

### 🥉 Initiate
- **How to earn:** Open any merged PR.
- **Description:** You've started your journey with the playbook.

### 🥈 Practitioner
- **How to earn:** Submit a PR that modifies `promptfooconfig.yaml` or `eval.py`.
- **Description:** You've applied the evaluation practices.

### 🥇 Sage
- **How to earn:** Add a new file in `docs/03-design/adr/` or `docs/07-operations/runbooks/`.
- **Description:** You've contributed to architecture or operations knowledge.

### 🌟 Stargazer
- **How to earn:** Star the repository.
- **Description:** You're spreading the word about the playbook.

## Viewing Your Badges

- Check the `LEADERBOARD.md` for current rankings.
- Badges update daily via automated action.
- See `docs/00-onboarding/gamification.md` for more details.
